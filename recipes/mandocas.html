Mandocas

Description

Zulian's sweet appetizer: fried and made from corn, cheese and plantains

Ingredients

  • Corn flour
  • Cheese/li>
  • Plantains
  • Water
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Mix the corn flour with water and salt
  2. Cook the plantains and then mash them
  3. Add the mashed plantains and the cheese to the mix
  4. Shape the mix like pretzels and cook in oil until crisp

Arepas

Description

Venezuela's national dish: a corn cake, baked and stuffed with chicken mixed with avocado

Ingredients

  • Corn flour
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Chicken breast
  • Avocado
  • Lemon

Steps

  1. Mix salt in water
  2. Add corn flour to water
  3. Mix until consistency
  4. Make cake shape and set to pan until dry
  5. For the stuffing: chicken, just boil and then shred
  6. Mix shredded chickem with mashed avocado and a squish of lemon
